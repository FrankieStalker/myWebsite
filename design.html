GGJ 2022: Dual Plane

This year I participated in the Global Game Jam where me and 3 others created a small 2D, side scrolling puzzle game where the player controls two characters switching between them to complete tasks to help the other progress. I was tasked with creating original 2D assets for the game as well as rigging the character. I had a lot of fun creating these assets over the 48 hours as I did have limited time trying to balance this with my part-time job but I am very pleased with the outcome.
